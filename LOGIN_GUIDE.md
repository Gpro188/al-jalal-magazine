# AL-JALAL Magazine - Simple Login Guide

## 🎯 For Students (Contributors)

### How to Login:

1. **Go to the login page**: Visit `/login`
2. **Select your Class Union**: Click on your union's card (e.g., Alpha Union, Beta Union, etc.)
3. **Enter your credentials**:
   - Email: Your student email
   - Password: Your password (provided by your union representative)
4. **Click "Sign In"**

### What You Can Do:
- ✍️ Write and submit articles
- 📝 Edit your pending submissions
- 👀 View your submission status
- 📚 Read published magazine content

---

## ✏️ For Editors

### How to Login:

1. **Go to the login page**: Visit `/login`
2. **Click "Editor/Staff Login"** button at the bottom
3. **Enter your credentials**:
   - Email: Your editor email
   - Password: Your password (provided by admin)
4. **Click "Sign In"**

### What You Can Do:
- ✅ Review student submissions
- ✔️ Approve or reject articles
- 📊 Manage posts from your assigned unions
- 💬 Provide feedback to contributors

---

## 🔑 For Administrators

### How to Login:

1. **Go to the login page**: Visit `/login`
2. **Click "Editor/Staff Login"** button at the bottom
3. **Enter admin credentials**:
   - Email: admin@school.edu (or your admin email)
   - Password: Your admin password
4. **Click "Sign In"**

### What You Can Do:
- 👥 Create and manage editor accounts
- 🏛️ Create and manage class unions
- 📰 Publish approved articles
- 🖼️ Add featured images
- ⚙️ Full system management

---

## 🆕 Getting Started (For New Students)

### Step 1: Get Your Account
Contact your **Class Union Representative** or **School Administrator** to create your account.

You'll need to provide:
- Your full name
- Your email address
- Your class union

### Step 2: Receive Credentials
You'll receive:
- Your login email
- A temporary password

### Step 3: First Login
1. Go to `/login`
2. Select your union
3. Login with your credentials
4. Change your password if needed

---

## 💡 Tips for Students

### Writing Good Articles:
1. **Choose a category**: Literature, Science, Arts, Sports, etc.
2. **Write clearly**: Use proper grammar and spelling
3. **Be original**: Write your own content
4. **Add details**: Make your article engaging
5. **Review before submitting**: Check for errors

### After Submission:
- Your article will be marked as "Pending Review"
- An editor from your union will review it
- You'll be notified when it's approved or if changes are needed
- Once approved by an editor, admin will publish it

---

## ❓ Common Issues

### "Invalid email or password"
- Double-check your email and password
- Make sure Caps Lock is off
- Contact your union representative if you forgot your password

### "Account not found"
- Your account might not be created yet
- Contact your Class Union Representative
- Or contact the school administrator

### Can't see my union?
- Refresh the page
- Contact admin if your union is missing

---

## 📞 Need Help?

- **Students**: Contact your Class Union Representative
- **Editors**: Contact the School Administrator
- **Technical Issues**: Contact the IT Support Team

---

## 🔐 Security Notes

- Never share your password with others
- Each student has their own individual account
- Passwords should be at least 6 characters
- Logout when using shared computers
