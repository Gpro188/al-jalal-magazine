✅ CHANGED: Union icons can now be PNG logos!

WHAT'S NEW:
-----------
1. Admin panel now has "Union Logo (PNG)" upload field
2. Logos stored in Firebase Storage automatically
3. Login pages show logo instead of emoji
4. Falls back to emoji if no logo uploaded

HOW TO USE:
-----------
Step 1: Login as admin at http://localhost:3002/login
        Email: admin@school.edu
        Password: Admin123!

Step 2: Go to Manage Panel → http://localhost:3002/dashboard/admin/manage

Step 3: Click "Manage Class Unions" tab

Step 4: Create/Edit Union:
        - Fill name, color, icon (emoji fallback)
        - Click "Union Logo (PNG)" field
        - Upload your logo file (PNG or JPG)
        - Preview appears automatically
        - Click "Create Class Union"

Step 5: Test it!
        - Go to http://localhost:3002/login/alpha
        - You'll see the Alpha Union logo!

LOGO REQUIREMENTS:
------------------
Format: PNG (best) or JPG
Size: 200x200 pixels recommended
Background: Transparent (PNG) or solid
File Size: Under 500KB
